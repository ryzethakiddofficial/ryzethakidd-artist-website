H3ART

“H3ART” by Ryze Tha Kidd is an electrifying hyperpop love anthem that pulses with raw emotion and high-octane energy. The track kicks off with a dramatic confession—giving one's heart to the devil—ushering in a sense of chaotic freedom, where reality glitches and the rules fade away. The protagonist knows there’s no future in this love, yet they dive headfirst into the present, craving the intensity and connection that comes with it. It’s a song about living fully in the moment, without hesitation or pretense, and hoping the wild ride never ends. With an infectious blend of soaring beats and poignant lyrics, “H3ART” captures the thrilling, bittersweet highs of a love that’s both all-consuming and fleeting.
