4LW4YS B3 B3TT3R

“4LW4YS B3 B3TT3R” by Ryze Tha Kidd is a bold and assertive track that showcases the artist’s confidence and competitive nature. The lyrics boast about outperforming others, particularly in romantic pursuits, with a provocative and unapologetic tone. The repetitive chorus highlights this bravado, while a brief interlude delves into themes of longing and love, adding a contrasting layer of emotional depth. The song merges aggressive self-assurance with moments of vulnerability, reflecting a multifaceted approach to relationships and personal success.
