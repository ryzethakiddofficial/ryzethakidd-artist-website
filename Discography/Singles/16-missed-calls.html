16 Missed Calls

​"16 Missed Calls" by Ryze Tha Kidd is a compelling track that delves into themes of unrequited love and emotional turmoil. The repetitive chorus, "Why you ignoring all my calls, 16 missed calls," underscores Ryze's frustration and longing for connection. The lyrics vividly depict the intensity of these feelings, with imagery of love and despair intertwined: "I just want to say that I loved you and all, Your face plastered all over the walls, My blood plastered all over the halls." The song conveys a deep desire to both embrace and possess, encapsulated in the lines, "I want to hold you, And control you." This track captures the raw emotion of heartache and the complexities of passionate love.
