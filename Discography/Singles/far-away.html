Far Away

​“Far Away” is a soulful and heartfelt R&B love song that expresses deep emotions of commitment and closeness. The lyrics convey a sense of unwavering devotion, promising that no matter the distance or time, the bond between Ryze Tha Kidd and his girlfriend will remain strong. With its slow tempo and smooth, mellow vibes, the song captures the essence of love, loyalty, and the comfort of knowing someone will always be there. Its intimate and tender tone makes it a beautiful track for those who cherish lasting connections.
