About Me

Ryan William Duncuft (15th September 2007), known professionally as Ryze Tha Kidd (pronounced "rise the kid") is a British Rapper and singer-songwriter.


Early Life

Duncuft began his career in 2019 recording songs on a cellphone with a cheap pair of earphones on the music recording app Bandlab. He later moved to FL Studio and began making bigger productions. He has also produced some of his own songs.

Ryan William Duncuft was born on September 15 2007 in Manchester, UK. He grew up in a town North East of the city centre called Mossley in the borough of Tameside. He grew up with his mother and his two siblings. His influence was crafted by Rock, Alternative and Hip-Hop & Trap.
