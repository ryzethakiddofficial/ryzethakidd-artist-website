About Me

Ryan William Duncuft (15th September, 2007), known professionally as Ryze Tha Kidd (pronounced "rise the kid"), is an upcoming British rapper and singer/songwriter from Mossley, Greater Manchester, UK. His name was derived from his ambition to become the best and 'rise' to the top, hence the name Ryze.

Duncuft began his career as an independent artist in 2019 under the name Myth!. His most popular song is his single "Collide". He collaborated with artist Okbreezy on his debut EP, "Is This A Dream?" (2024) and released his debut album, "Healing Wounds" (2024), which contained "Scars" featuring "Emilyn".

Early Life

Ryan William Duncuft was born on 15th September 2007 in Manchester, UK. He grew up in Mossley, a suburb of Greater Manchester, where he studied at Mossley Hollins High School and eventually graduated in 2024. He grew up listening to Rock, late 90's and early 00's party and rap. These genres all work together in Ryze's music and combine to create his distinct sound. He stumbled upon the rapper Juice WRLD's music in early 2018 and became a fan since that day and he attributes his sound to the late rapper.

He has always been obsessed with music since a young age. He has a passion for old music as well as modern Trap. He learned to play Guitar and the piano at the age of 11. In Year 8 of High school, he began to post songs to YouTube and Soundcloud that he recorded on the app, "BandLab".

Artistry

Duncuft said his musical influences were genre-wide from emo, hip hop music, elements of rock, punk, and R&B, and that his biggest influences were rappers, "Juice WRLD", "XXXTENTACION", "Eminem", and "Aitch".

His music has been labelled as "emo" with music focusing on "every broken heart, every wounded feeling". More specifically, he has been labeled as a hip hop, emo rap, trap, and SoundCloud rap artist. Duncuft switches between writing and freestyling his songs, leaning more towards freestyling to give a more immersive sound.
