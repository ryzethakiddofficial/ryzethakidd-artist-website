About Ryze Tha Kidd

Ryan William Duncuft(born 15th September 2007), professionally known asRyze Tha Kidd, is a British rapper and singer-songwriter from Manchester, UK. He is known for his unique blend of hip hop, emo rap, and trap music.


Early Life

Ryan William Duncuft was born in Manchester and raised in Mossley, a town north-east of the city centre in the borough of Tameside. Growing up with his mother and two siblings, Duncuft's early musical influences were rooted in rock, alternative, hip hop, and trap genres.

Duncuft's musical journey began in 2019 when he started recording music using a basic cellphone and cheap earphones through the BandLab app. As his passion for music grew, he transitioned to FL Studio, producing more advanced tracks and gaining recognition for his raw talent. He has also taken on the role of a music producer, crafting several of his own songs.


Career

Ryze Tha Kidd's rise in the music scene began with a distinct sound blending various genres. Over the years, his music has evolved, reflecting his diverse influences and personal experiences. As of 2024, Ryze Tha Kidd continues to make waves in the UK music scene with multiple successful releases under his label, Ryze Collective Media Group.
